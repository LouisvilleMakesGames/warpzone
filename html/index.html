A Whole New Zone!

If you are familiar with the original Warp Zone Louisville location the new location is right down the alley, literally 5 doors down! We are now in the Fund For the Arts building in downtown Louisville, in historic Museum Row.

We here at Louisville makes Games(the 501(c)(3) non-profit which runs Warp Zone Louisville) are very excited to start year 5 of providing Kentucky’s only game development-focused collaborative workspace and community space, self-funded entirely by the community and co-working members!

Become a member

Our community of developers and other creative folks are special to us. We are more than a co-working space, but an inclusive community of creative collaborators, so we are selective with our memberships for the sake and comfort of our existing community. We think you’ll like that about us.

Work on your schedule

Our members enjoy access to Warp Zone Louisville 24 hours a day, seven days a week. So you can get an early start, or stay as late as you want, however you want to look at it, we’ve got space for you.
